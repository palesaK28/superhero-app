ANIMALS MAP PLACE

GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth, known for their long necks and legs. They primarily feed on leaves from tall trees.

ELEPHANT

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and long trunks.

LION

Lions are known as the "king of the jungle." They live in groups called prides and are famous for their majestic manes.

PANDA

Pandas are known for their distinctive black and white fur. They primarily eat bamboo and are native to China.

GEMSBOK

Gemsboks are large antelopes native to southern Africa. They are known for their striking appearance and long, straight horns.

TIGER

Tigers are the largest cat species in the world. They are known for their powerful build and striking orange coat with black stripes.

MONKEY

Monkeys are highly intelligent primates known for their playful behavior and social structures. They are found in various habitats around the world.

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest primates and are known for their strong social bonds. They live in family groups and are primarily herbivorous.

KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia. They are known for their tree-dwelling lifestyle and diet primarily consisting of eucalyptus leaves.

WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild pigs native to Africa. They are known for their distinctive tusks and are often found in savannahs and grasslands.
